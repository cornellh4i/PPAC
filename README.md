<h2>PPAC (Pelvic Pain Association at Cornell)</h2>
  <p>
    The Pelvic Pain Association at Cornell (PPAC) is a student-led organization whose mission is to improve awareness, access to care, and support for students experiencing pelvic pain–related conditions at Cornell University. PPAC aims to make Cornell a more accessible and inclusive campus by advocating for better pelvic pain resources, increasing transparency around available care, and fostering open conversations around reproductive and pelvic health.
